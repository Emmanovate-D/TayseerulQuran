What You Will Learn:

This comprehensive course will guide you through the fundamentals of Qur'anic studies, covering proper recitation techniques, Tajweed rules, and Arabic language foundations. You will learn to read the Qur'an with correct pronunciation and understanding.

  • Proper Arabic letter pronunciation (Makharij al-Huruf)
  • Basic Tajweed rules and their application
  • Reading fluency and rhythm (Tarteel)
  • Understanding Arabic script and diacritical marks
  • Memorization techniques (Hifdh methods)

Course Completion Certificate:

Upon successful completion of this course, you will receive a recognized certificate of completion. This certificate validates your understanding and proficiency in Qur'anic recitation and Tajweed rules, which can be used for further Islamic studies or teaching purposes.

The certificate includes verification of your completion status and can be downloaded from your student dashboard after finishing all course modules and assessments.
